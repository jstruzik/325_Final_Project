Your First Stop For UMass Textbook Supplies

Stop waiting weeks and reaching deep into your wallet for expensive textbooks this semester. Ubooks is a program local to the UMass community where students can borrow/buy or lend/sell textbooks for any of their classes.

Step One

Sign up for our service and search for the book(s) you're trying to buy,borrow,lend or sell.

Step Two

Pick up or drop off your selected book at the UBooks center on campus.

Step Three

Once the semester is over, drop off any borrowed books and collect lent books or money from sold books.
